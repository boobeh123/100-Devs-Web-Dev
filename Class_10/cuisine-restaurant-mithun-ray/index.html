About us

Providing a pleasant experience

Here at Itadaki, we support local industries by sourcing our produce on island. We strive to serve the highest quality entrees for a family-friendly price.

Our broth is cooked for several hours with a variety of meats. This allows us to serve natural ingredients instead of artificial flavors.

We prioritize the well-being of our employees and maintain high retention. This leads to an experienced workforce, increased job satisfaction, and in turn an excellent experience for you.
